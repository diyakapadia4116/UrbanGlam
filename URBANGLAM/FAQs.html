Frequently Asked Questions


What payment methods do you accept?
We accept UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery (COD).

How long does delivery take?
Delivery usually takes 3 to 7 working days depending on your location.

Can I return or exchange an item?
Yes! You can return or exchange an item within 7 days of delivery, as long as it meets our return policy conditions.

Is COD (Cash on Delivery) available?
Yes, COD is available in selected pin codes. You’ll see the option at checkout if it’s applicable to your area.

How can I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via SMS or email.
